Privacy Policy for Edison

Effective date: March 23, 2026

Edison is a personal hobby project operated by David Washington. This Privacy Policy explains how Edison collects, uses, and protects information when sending SMS messages related to personal project alerts, test notifications, and device or system updates.

Information We Collect

Edison may collect:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • SMS opt-in and opt-out records
  • Message delivery and interaction records
  • Basic technical or account information needed to operate the service

How We Use Information

Edison uses this information only to:

  • Send SMS alerts and test messages you requested
  • Maintain opt-in and opt-out records
  • Operate, troubleshoot, and improve the Edison hobby project

Sharing of Information

Edison does not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Mobile opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Edison may use service providers strictly as needed to operate messaging functionality, such as Twilio for SMS delivery.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on project activity, alerts, and testing.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opting Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP.

Help

For help, reply HELP or contact dwashin@outlook.com.

Data Retention

Edison keeps data only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the project, maintain messaging records, and comply with applicable requirements.

Security

Edison uses reasonable measures to protect the information it stores, but no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

Intended Use

Edison is a personal hobby project and is intended only for the project owner or explicitly approved testers who have opted in.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
